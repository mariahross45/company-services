Our Services

Subscription Delivery

Eating well should be easy and stress-free. Our weekly subscription delivery service brings fresh, ready-to-make meal kits or healthy snacks straight to your door. Customers can choose flexible plans that fit their schedule and budget, whether that’s three meals a week, daily lunches, or just snack boxes to keep at the office.

Healthy Lifestyle Options

At Khaana, we believe everyone deserves access to meals that fit their lifestyle. That’s why we offer vegetarian, vegan, and keto-friendly options that are carefully crafted to be both nutritious and flavorful. Whether you’re cutting carbs, avoiding animal products, or just looking for more plant-based choices, our chefs design meals that never compromise on taste.

Customized Meal Plans + Tutorials

We know that every customer is unique. That’s why we offer personalized meal plans that can be tailored to specific needs such as gluten-free, vegetarian, vegan, or cultural cuisines. To make the experience even better, we also provide step-by-step recipes and online video tutorials with every meal kit. This ensures customers not only enjoy healthy food but also learn how to prepare it with confidence.
